How FollowDiff Works

Everything you need to track Instagram follower changes, all running locally in your browser.

Features

Built for privacy and simplicity

Save Snapshots

Capture a profile's complete follower and following list at any moment. Save as many snapshots as you need.

Compare Changes

Select any two snapshots and instantly see who followed, unfollowed, or was removed between them.

Export Data

Download snapshots as JSON or export follower lists as plain text. Your data, your format.

100% Private

All data stays in your browser's local storage. Nothing is ever sent to external servers.

Track Multiple Profiles

Monitor as many Instagram accounts as you want. Each profile has its own snapshot history.

Fast Fetching

Efficiently fetches follower data using Instagram's web APIs. Optional slow mode for large accounts.

How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Install the Extension

Add FollowDiff to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store. It's completely free and takes just seconds to install.

2

Take a Snapshot

Visit any Instagram profile or enter a username in the dashboard. Click Start to save a complete snapshot of their followers and following.

3

Compare Over Time

Come back later, take another snapshot, and compare any two to see exactly who followed or unfollowed.

Your data stays with you

FollowDiff runs entirely in your browser. All snapshots are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. We have no servers, no accounts, and absolutely no access to your data.
