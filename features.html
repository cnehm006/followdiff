How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Install the Extension

Add FollowDiff to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store. It's completely free and takes just seconds to install.

2

Take a Snapshot

Visit any Instagram profile or enter a username in the dashboard. Click Start to save a complete snapshot of their followers and following.

3

Compare Over Time

Come back later, take another snapshot, and compare any two to see exactly who followed or unfollowed.

Your data stays with you

FollowDiff runs entirely in your browser. All snapshots are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. We have no servers, no accounts, and absolutely no access to your data.
